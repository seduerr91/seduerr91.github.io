The latest updates to the Sora API, announced in March 2026, focus on empowering users with improved creative tools for video generation. Notable enhancements include support for consistent character references, higher-resolution exports (up to 1080p), longer video durations (up to 20 seconds), and advanced video extension capabilities. The posts collectively emphasize the importance of detailed and specific prompts, while also promoting creative exploration and iterative refinement. Comprehensive guidance is now available on prompt structuring, covering parameters such as camera setups, lighting, and actions, as well as leveraging image inputs for greater visual control. Overall, the updates signal a trend toward greater flexibility, precision, and user agency in AI-driven video creation.
