OpenAI has released a guide for its latest image generation model, **gpt-image-2**, designed for high-quality visual production and creative workflows. Key features include high-fidelity photorealism, flexible quality-latency trade-offs, robust facial identity preservation, and reliable text rendering. The model excels at generating complex visuals and offers precise style control with minimal prompting. 

OpenAI recommends using **gpt-image-2** for most production tasks due to its superior quality, and advises teams using older models to migrate for improved performance. The guide provides detailed prompting strategies to optimize output quality, covering aspects such as composition, text embedding, and responsiveness to user constraints. It also outlines best practices for generating specific visual types, including infographics and logo designs, enhancing both user experience and efficiency in creative processes.
