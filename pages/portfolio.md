- **Message Understanding and Insights at Cordial:** shipped services that analyzed millions of marketing messages for calls to action and other content signals, including insights from roughly one billion emails sent during the Black Friday and Cyber Monday period.

- **Persuaide, Resonaid, and Kindest AI:** founded and built a suite of full-stack AI products focused on persuasive writing and communication, supported by German and European startup scholarships.
