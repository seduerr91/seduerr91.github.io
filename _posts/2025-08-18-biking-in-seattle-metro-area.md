Here is a checklist of essential items for biking in the Seattle Metro Area:

- Charging Cable
- Sunglasses
- Gloves (wind is cold)
- Wind breaker (wind is cold)
- Little saddle bag
- Air pump
- Repair kit
- Water bottles 
- Bike charged (ahead of time)
- Bike locks
- Treats/energy, if we gets tired
- Map / route preloaded on Strava app
